FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a Dutch bank account or BSN to get started?
No. You do not need a Dutch bank account or a BSN (citizen service number) to sign a lease or to work with us. A simple wire transfer is enough to pay the deposit and the fee. We help many clients who are still abroad and do not yet have any Dutch documents.
How do I pay the deposit and the fee?
By wire transfer. You can pay from any bank account in the world. We will send you an invoice with our bank details after the intake call, once we have agreed to work together.
How long does it usually take to find a home?
On average, about three and a half weeks. Some searches are faster, some take longer. It depends on your budget, requirements, and the time of year. We will give you an honest estimate during the intake call.
What if I am still abroad?
No problem at all. Most of our clients find and sign their home before they arrive in Amsterdam. We arrange remote viewings, handle everything with landlords and agents, and make sure your home is ready when you land.
What documents do I need?
Typically you need a valid ID or passport, an employment contract or proof of income, and recent payslips. We will check all your documents before we start the search so there are no surprises later.
Can I bring my pet?
Yes. Many landlords in Amsterdam accept pets. We will make sure to only search for properties that are pet-friendly if you have a furry companion.
